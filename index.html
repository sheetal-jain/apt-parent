APT Parents Section

Welcome to the

APT-Parents

section!

The additional Prevention Center (APC-CQLD) offers this program to help you effectively deal with with your teenagers to prevent/reduce their consumption of the psychoactive substances (alcohol, marijuana and other drugs).

The APT-Parents section is comprised of four capsules for a total duration of 60 minuts. Each capsules cover an aspect of parental responsibility that helps reduce the risk of consumption among adolescents:

A Meaningful relationship beetween parents and abolescents

Effective communication between parents and abolescents

Good parenting practices (discipline)

Appropriate conflict resolution strategies

The Smith Family
meet the smith family

In each capsule, you will help parents John and Jennifer resolve problematic situations involving psychoactive substances and their teenagers, Michael and Emma.

You will also help them demystify any beliefs their teenagers have about alcohol, marijuana and other drugs.

Gather Resources!

At the end of each capsule, you will receive one or more information sheets about the parental responsibility covered in the capsule: meaningful relationships, communication, parenting practices and conflict resolution. These sheets will also be available in the Resources section as you complete the capsules.

You can also consult the document on common beliefs and misconceptions about psychoactive substances, available at all times in the Resources section.
